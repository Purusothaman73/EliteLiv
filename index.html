Elite Liv has Branches at Perumbakam, Madipakkam and Karapakkam

Elite Liv was founded on 10th March 2023, with a vision to provide affordable, high-quality living spaces for students, working professionals, and travelers. With three strategically located branches in Perumbakam, Madipakkam, and Karapakkam, we offer fully furnished flats that combine comfort, convenience, and security.

Our facilities include: Fully Furnished Flats with essential appliances, Unlimited WiFi for your work and leisure needs, CCTV Surveillance for added security, Power Backup ensuring uninterrupted service, Hot Water Facility available round the clock, Fridge, Washing Machine, and Induction Stove for ease of living, RO Drinking Water Purifier for clean water, AC Rooms, Cot, Mattress, and Individual Wardrobes for a homely experience, Attached Washrooms for privacy and convenience. At Elite Liv, we focus on providing a comfortable and secure environment, allowing our residents to focus on what matters most — whether it's studying, working, or relaxing.
